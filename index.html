About Rooted

At Rooted, we believe that a healthy scalp is the foundation of healthy hair. Your scalp’s well-being directly impacts your hair growth, and we’re here to help you nourish it naturally. Combining AI-driven insights with expert-backed guidance, we provide personalized home remedies tailored to combat hair loss and promote stronger, healthier hair growth.

Whether you're struggling with hair loss, scalp dryness, dandruff, or scalp irritation, Rooted offers solutions designed to restore balance and revitalize your scalp. Our mission is to empower you with knowledge and natural remedies so you can take charge of your hair health—right from the comfort of your home.

Let’s grow healthier, stronger hair — starting from the roots.
